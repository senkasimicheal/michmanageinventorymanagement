Privacy Policy

1. Introduction: This Privacy Policy outlines our commitment to protecting the privacy of personal information provided by property managers, tenants and clients using our property management system.

2. Collection of Information: We collect personal information necessary for the effective management of tenant payments and property maintenance. This may include names, contact details, payment history, and other relevant data.

3. Use of Information: The information collected will be used solely for the purposes of property management, including rent collection, maintenance requests, and communication regarding tenancy matters.

4. Communication: We may use your contact information to send you updates, newsletters, marketing communications, or respond to your inquiries.

5. Disclosure of Information: Personal information will not be disclosed to third parties without consent, except where required by law or to provide necessary services related to property management or with your explicit consent.

6. Data Security: We implement robust security measures to protect personal information from unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction.

7. Access and Correction: Property managers and clients have the right to access and correct their personal information held by us.

8. Changes to the Policy: We reserve the right to modify this privacy policy at any time, so please review it frequently. Any changes will be posted on our website with the effective date.

9. Your Privacy Choices: Please contact us at the email provided in the contact info for any inquiries or requests regarding your data.

10. Contact Us: If you have questions, concerns, or requests regarding this Privacy Policy or our data practices, please contact us using the contact information provided on the homepage.
