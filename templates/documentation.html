User Menu

Accessing Menu Items

To open the menu items, click on the icon located in the top right corner of the screen, as indicated by the red arrow in the picture below.

Menu Items

The menu list includes the following items: Home, Manager Login, Tenant Login, Manager Sign Up, Tenant Sign Up, Privacy Policy, Terms of Service, About, and Contact Us as shown in the picture below.

User Login

Manager/Landlord Login

To log in as a property manager or landlord, click on 'Manager Login' in the menu, as indicated below in the picture.

When 'Manager Login' is clicked, the form below will appear, requiring your username and password. After entering them, click on the login button to continue.

Tenant Login

To log in as a tenant, click on 'Tenant Login' in the menu, as indicated below in the picture.

When 'Tenant Login' is clicked, the form below will appear, requiring your username and password. After entering them, click on the login button to continue.

User Registration

Manager/Landlord Registration

To register an account, click on 'Manager Sign Up' as indicated in the picture below.

When 'Manager Sign Up' is clicked, a registration page will be loaded and user can provide the required feedback

Tenant Registration

To register as a tenant, click on 'Tenant Sign Up' as indicated in the picture below.

When 'Tenant Sign Up' is clicked, a registration page will be loaded and user can provide the required feedback

Property Management

Dashboard

To load the property management dashboard, click on the Overview button in the menu on the left-hand side, as indicated in the picture below.

To customize the period of what is viewed on the dashboard, enter the start date and end date, then click on Apply, as indicated by arrows 1, 2, and 3 respectively.

Add Property

To add properties to be managed on your account, click on the Add Property button in the menu, as indicated in the picture below.

Fill in all the fields in the property form below, then click on add to submit property information.

Add Tenant

To add tenant to the properties on your account, click on the Add Tenants button in the menu, as indicated in the picture below.

Fill in all the fields in the tenant form below, then click on add to submit tenant information.

Tenants Information

To view, update, edit, and delete tenant data from your account, click on Tenants, as indicated in the picture below.

Sample Tenant Data

Update Tenant Data

To update tenant data, such as payments, click anywhere on the tenant. This action will load an update form that needs to be filled out, as indicated in the picture below.

Edit Tenant Data

To edit tenant data, including payments, click on the edit button indicated by arrow 3 on the tenant row. This action will load an edit form that needs to be filled out, as shown in the pictures below.

Delete Tenant From Property

To delete tenant data from the property, click on the delete button indicated by arrow 4 in the picture below.

View Rent Payment Receipt

To view the payment receipt, click on the view button in the tenant row, as indicated by arrow 2 in the picture below. This action will download a receipt with a scannable QR code.

Filter Tenants

To filter and view specific tenants, type the tenant name in the filter field indicated by arrow 1 in the picture below.

Property Information

To view and update property data on your account, click on 'Properties,' as indicated in the picture below.

Sample Property Data

Update Property Data

To update property data, such as property value, click anywhere on the property as indicated by the arrow in the picture above. This action will load an update form that needs to be filled out, as shown in the picture below.

Tenant Complaints

To view both resolved and unresolved complaints from tenants, click on Complaints in the menu as indicated in the picture below

Unresolved Complaints

To load unresolved complaints, click on 'Pending' as indicated by arrow 1 in the picture below. Reply to a complaint by filling in your reply and clicking the 'Reply' button indicated by arrow 2. To view replies from both tenants and managers, click on the 'Show Replies' button indicated by arrow 3. If an agreement is reached between the tenant and manager, click on the 'Resolve' button to mark the complaint as resolved. It will then disappear from 'Pending' and move to 'Resolved'.

Resolved Complaints

To view all resolved complaints, click on the 'Resolved' button, as indicated by the arrow in the picture below.

Contracts

Add Contract

To add a contract, click on the add button in the menu as indicated in the first picture below which will load the add contract page to be filled and submitted as indicated in the second picture below.

Add contract page

View Contracts

To view the contracts signed between the tenant and the property manager or landlord, click on Contracts in the menu as indicated in the picture below

Update Contract

To update a contract, click anywhere on the row for that contract. This action will load the update contract page, as shown in the pictures below.

Contract update page

Download Contract

To download a contract, click on the download button in the row for that contract, as indicated by arrow 1 in the picture below.

Delete Contract

To delete a contract, click on the delete button in the row for that contract, as indicated by arrow 2 in the picture below.

Export Tenant Data

To export tenant data, click on the export button, as indicated in the picture below.

In the export form, specify the start and end times for the data to be exported. A password-protected Excel file will be downloaded once the export button is clicked.

To view the file password for the exported data, click on 'Passwords,' as indicated in the picture below.

File passwords for the exported data are shown, with arrow 2 indicating a password.

User Settings
